The Circuit Breakers Final Project

This website is a collaboration of all four of the group members including Caleb, Max, Aidan, and Jeremy.
For our project we were tasked with designing our own wind turbine blades and testing the power output in a wind tunnel.
This project helped us further undertsand how to document our progress while using the scientific method to identify our faults while in the trial and error stage of testing.
Each of the links below will direct you to each one of our individual designs, our trial and errors, and the progress of the project.
Also attached is our ethics project as well as our data that we collected from the wind tunnel test.
