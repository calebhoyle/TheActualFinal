Project Overview

Renewable energy is an everlasting necessity for the world seeing as more and more technology is being developed and as it increases in both quantity and magnitude, more energy is needed to power said technology.
A VAWT (Vertical Axis Wind Turbine) rotates on a vertical axis and is used to efficiently generate energy in low wind areas whereas a HAWT (Horizontal Axis Wind Turbine) rotates on a horizontal axis and is mainly used in dedicated wind farms.

Design Process

In a HAWT, the main components would most likely be the coils, along with the magnets and fans alongside it. The coils are wires bundled together in a circular shape and used for a current when affected by a magnetic field created by
the magnets that surround it in a wind turbine. Voltage is generated when the shaft connected to the magnets is spun fast enough to create an electric current. This speed can be reached when adding fans to the other end of the shaft.
